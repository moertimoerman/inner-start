# Product Vision

Inner Sleep is a calm, low-friction sleep audio product for children, designed to help parents create a repeatable bedtime routine that supports feelings of safety, calm, and inner strength.

## Core Product Intent

- Help children settle into sleep with a calm spoken routine.
- Reinforce inner safety, self-worth, and resilience through repeated exposure.
- Keep the experience simple for parents and soothing for children.

## MVP Direction

- The MVP should prove that the standard audio experience is valuable and operationally manageable.
- The product should avoid expensive real-time generation in the standard path.
- Premium can be prepared as a future personalized layer, but should not destabilize the MVP.

## Product Tone

- calm
- clear
- grounded
- parent-facing
- not hype-driven

## Commercial Intent

- Standard should be easy to understand and low-friction.
- Premium should feel meaningfully better only when personalization is real and justified.
- Conversion improvements should preserve trust.
