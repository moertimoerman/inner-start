# Launch Checklist Playbook

## Checklist

- Locked decisions reviewed
- Standard audio assets present and correctly referenced
- Auth flow works
- Pricing and checkout paths match configured plans
- Webhook path and subscription update path are documented
- Protected app gating behaves correctly
- Product claims match the implemented product
- Known MVP limitations are documented
- No unnecessary experimental paths are exposed to users
- Final QA report completed

## Focus

- stability
- clarity
- low operational risk
- low support burden
